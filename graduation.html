Important Notes

  • Kindly Collect your transcripts and degree certificates from the Limuru Campus Registry Office from From 7th January – 9th July 2021 to avoid a fee of Kshs. 100 per day inclusive of weekends, up to a maximum of Kshs. 6000.
  • Please remember to bring your original National ID/Passport. If you are unable to come personally, authorize (in writing) the person allowed to collect your certificate including their National ID number. The person will be required to come with a copy of your ID plus their original ID.
  • If you are an International student, your certificate can be sent by courier if you provide your physical address and telephone contact. You will be charged for courier services.
  • Graduation attire should be returned before 16th October 2020 to avoid charges of Kshs.350 a day inclusive of weekends
